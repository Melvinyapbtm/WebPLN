Services

DISMANTLING & INSTALLING POWER PLANT

Dismantling and installing a power plant involves careful planning, specialized equipment, and skilled technicians to ensure the safe removal of old infrastructure and the efficient setup of new systems.

MECHANICAL & ELECTRICAL

Mechanical and electrical systems are essential components in modern infrastructure, with mechanical engineering focusing on the design and maintenance of machines, while electrical engineering handles power generation, distribution, and control systems.

BUILDING AND POWER PLANT CONSTRUCTION

Mechanical and electrical engineering play crucial roles in various industries, with mechanical focusing on machinery, structures, and thermal systems, while electrical covers power systems, circuitry, and control technologies.

POWER PLANT MAINTENANCE

Power plant maintenance is vital to ensure the continuous operation of energy generation systems, involving regular inspections, repairs, and upgrades to maintain efficiency and prevent downtime.

POWER PLANT OPERATION

Power plant operation involves the management and control of equipment to efficiently generate electricity, ensuring that all systems run smoothly and safely to meet energy demands.

REPAIR

Repair involves diagnosing and fixing issues in equipment or systems to restore their functionality and prevent further damage or failure.

MANPOWER SUPPLY

Manpower supply refers to providing skilled workers and labor resources to meet the demands of various projects, ensuring that tasks are completed efficiently and on time.

GENERAL SUPPLY

General supply involves providing a wide range of goods and materials needed for operations, ensuring that all resources are available to support day-to-day activities and project requirements.

OIL & GAS SERVICES

Oil and gas services encompass a wide range of activities, from exploration and drilling to production and maintenance, ensuring the efficient and safe extraction and management of energy resources.
